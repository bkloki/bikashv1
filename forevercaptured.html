Forevercaptured

Description

So, I designed this wedding photography website — Forever Captured — with a modern, elegant vibe that feels both romantic and professional. I wanted the site to reflect the emotional and high-end nature of wedding photography, while keeping it super clean and easy to navigate The landing section has a full-screen background image (pulled from Unsplash) with a subtle dark overlay to make the white text pop. It’s a classic CTA layout — big, emotional headline, supporting copy, and a clear “Book a Session” button. The goal here is to grab attention fast and immediately communicate value. The primary palette is pink and white, which feels soft, romantic, and welcoming. I used TailwindCSS for styling — it’s fast and lets me work efficiently with utility classes. Fonts are kept clean and sans-serif to maintain modern readability. Everything is built to be fully responsive. The layout shifts from single-column on mobile to multi-column on desktops (especially for the Services, Testimonials, and Tools sections). There’s a sticky nav bar that collapses into a hamburger menu on smaller screens — using Tailwind's responsive utilities and a tiny bit of JS to toggle it.
